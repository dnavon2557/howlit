About

How Lit is a web app that lets a user generate a playlist based on a genre of music and how "lit" they want the playlist to be. The concept was originally concieved and implemented by Daniel Navon and Lauren Dierker at the Tufts Polyhack 2015. The current version of How Lit was rewritten from scratch by Daniel Navon.
